Our Mission

We exist to give founder-led companies the operational edge that used to belong only to the enterprise.

Most automation tools are built for companies with IT departments, implementation budgets, and dedicated software teams. You don't have that — and you shouldn't need it.

You built a business that works because you made it work. Adapted Machine builds AI automation that fits how you already operate, connects to the tools you already use, and runs without a team to manage it. You get the leverage. You keep the control.

We believe every founder deserves the same operational leverage as the Fortune 500.
