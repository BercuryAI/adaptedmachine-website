Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Adapted Machine ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at adaptedmachine.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site or contact us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you submit our contact form, including:

  • Your name and company name
  • A description of your operational challenge
  • An estimate of the business impact
  • Your email address or phone number

We also collect standard website analytics data (pages visited, referral source, browser type) through Google Analytics 4. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and evaluate whether we can help your business
  • Conduct the free diagnostic you requested
  • Follow up regarding a potential engagement

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain a business relationship. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing jack@adaptedmachine.com.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect aggregated, anonymous traffic data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Residents of California have additional rights under the CPRA. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jack@adaptedmachine.com.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree (formspree.io). Formspree's privacy policy applies to data submitted through our form. Google Analytics processes aggregated traffic data pursuant to Google's privacy policy.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure. Please do not submit sensitive business or financial information through our contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at jack@adaptedmachine.com.
